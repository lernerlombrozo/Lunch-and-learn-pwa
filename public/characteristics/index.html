Progressive Web Applications

Work in the background A PWA works in the background.

Installable A PWA can be installed in an android or IOS phone just like any native app.

Offline A PWA can work without internet connection just like any native app.

Push notifications A PWA can receive push notifications just like any native app.

Camera A PWA can open the device's camera just like any native app.

Geolocation A PWA can open the device's location just like any native app.
